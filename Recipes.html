Recipes


Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry the pancetta with the garlic until crisp.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with grated pecorino and parmesan.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and mix with the pancetta and garlic.
  5. Remove from heat and add the egg mixture, stirring quickly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve.

Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Chop the onion and garlic finely.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan and sauté the onion and garlic.
  4. Add the chicken and cook until browned.
  5. Sprinkle in the curry powder and cook for another minute.
  6. Add the coconut milk and simmer for 20 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve with rice.

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • Salt to taste
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic slices and red pepper flakes.
  3. Sauté until garlic is golden brown, being careful not to burn it.
  4. Drain spaghetti, reserving some pasta water.
  5. Add cooked spaghetti to the pan, tossing to coat in garlic oil. If it seems dry, add a bit of reserved pasta water.
  6. Season with salt, add chopped parsley, and toss again.
  7. Serve hot, topped with freshly grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
